FAQ

Common questions

How do I connect?

Pick a lane from Stratum Endpoints on the homepage. Username: <address>.workername Password: x.

When do I get paid?

Payout method and schedule shown on the Transparency page.

What fees do you charge?

2% pool fee.

Why is my hashrate different?

Pool vs. miner reporting can differ due to averaging windows and rejects.

What do “accepted” and “rejected” mean?

Accepted shares counted towards rewards; rejected are stale/invalid.

Where can I see my stats?

Use the search box on the homepage or open /miner.html?address=<your-address>.
